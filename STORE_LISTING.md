## 5. Long Description Draft

Tassulokero helps you keep your pet's daily care, important records and trusted helpers in one place.

Use Tassulokero to organize pet information, follow routines, track reminders and keep the right people up to date. Whether you manage one pet or several, the app is built to make everyday care easier and more reliable.

With Tassulokero you can:

- create and manage pet profiles
- store health and care information
- keep routines and care instructions easy to find
- track reminders and upcoming tasks
- share access with family members or caretakers
- keep a shared update history with notes and media
- support breeder-related links and records in the same app

Tassulokero is designed for real-life pet care:

- one place for reminders, records and routines
- better coordination between owners and trusted helpers
- role-based sharing so the right people see the right information
- room for both private care management and breeder-related records

Important:

- Tassulokero is an organizational tool and does not replace veterinary advice
- some features may vary as the service evolves toward the final v1 release
